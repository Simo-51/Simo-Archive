📖 How to Use Your Archive

Welcome to your personal archive! Here is a quick guide on how to get the most out of it:

🗂️ Organizing Tabs & Items

  • Drag and Drop: Click and hold any Tab or Item Card to drag it. You can reorder tabs, or change the order of items within a tab.
  • Tab Backgrounds: Click the small ⚙️ gear icon on any tab to change its name, give it a solid background color, or paste an image URL to customize your workspace.

🎬 Adding Multimedia & Previews

  • Auto-Previews: If you paste a YouTube link or a direct image link into the URL box, the system will automatically fetch the thumbnail/image for you. No need to fill out the "Override Image" box!
  • Media Viewer: If an item is a YouTube video, local video (mp4), audio file (mp3), or image, a "▶️ View" button will appear. Click it to view or play the file in a popup overlay without leaving the page.
  • Local Files: You can paste paths directly from your PC like "C:\Users\Photo.jpg". The system will automatically fix the formatting and remove the quotes for you.

🔍 Searching & Tags

  • Global Search: Typing in the top search bar searches everything across all tabs simultaneously.
  • Clickable Tags: Add comma-separated tags when creating an item. If you click a tag pill on a card, it instantly filters your entire archive for that specific tag.

⚠️ Important: Saving Your Data!

  • This archive runs on Local Storage. Your data is saved locally inside this specific web browser.
  • If you clear your browser's Cache/Site Data, or open this file in a different browser, your data will not be there.
  • Best Practice: Use the green Export Backup button regularly. This saves a physical file to your PC. If you ever lose your data, just click Import JSON to restore everything instantly!
